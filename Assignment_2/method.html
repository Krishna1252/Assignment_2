2. Method

2.1. Literature search and inclusion criteria

We followed guidelines set forth by Short (2009) and Tranfield et al. (2003) as illustrated in Fig. 1. We first searched several computerized databases between February and March 2021 to identify potential studies for inclusion: ABI/Inform, Business Source Complete, and Web of Science.

We used a Boolean search for studies that included the terms corporate*, organisation*, organization*, in combination with volunteer* or service-learning, and skill*, develop*, learn*, train* or educat* in the title, abstract, or keyword sections of peer-reviewed papers.

Since employee volunteering is a relatively new field of research, we did not restrict publication dates.
